Redemption

How to Redeem Your Gift Card

Redeeming couldn't be simpler. Your recipient has three ways to use their gift card balance:

1
Online booking — Enter the gift card code in the payment field on our booking page. The balance is applied instantly.
2
By phone — Call us on +91 80 4567 8901 and quote the code. Our team will apply the balance to the booking.
3
In-spa — Simply show the digital card or present the physical card at reception on the day of your visit.
Gift card balances can be used across multiple visits and combined with other gift cards. Remaining balances are automatically stored for future use. Cards are valid for 12 months from the purchase date and are non-refundable.
