This Privacy Policy applies to Serenity Spa (a sole-proprietorship registered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India) and governs all personal data collected through our website, booking system, in-spa consultations, loyalty programme, and newsletter service. This policy is compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and internationally aligned with GDPR principles.

01 Overview & Who We Are

Serenity Spa ("we", "us", "our") is a premium wellness and rejuvenation centre located at 14, MG Road, Indiranagar, Bengaluru – 560038. We provide massage therapy, facial treatments, body rituals, and holistic wellness services.

We act as the Data Fiduciary in relation to personal data you provide to us. We process your data only for the purposes described in this Policy and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Our guiding principle: We collect only what we genuinely need, protect it carefully, and give you meaningful control over it.

02 Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

Category What We Collect When
Identity Full name, date of birth (optional) Booking, loyalty sign-up
Contact Email address, phone number Booking, newsletter, contact form
Health Allergies, injuries, medical notes you share Pre-treatment consultation only
Transactional Treatment history, booking dates, package purchases Each booking
Technical IP address, browser type, pages visited, session duration Website visits (via cookies)
Feedback Reviews, survey responses, testimonials When you leave a review
Marketing Email preferences, campaign interactions Newsletter subscription (opt-in only)
Health data is treated with extra care. Information about allergies, injuries, or medical conditions shared during consultation is used only for treatment purposes, is never shared externally, and is retained only as long as needed for your ongoing care with us.

03 How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data on the following legal bases and for the following purposes:

  • To fulfil your bookings — confirming appointments, sending reminders, processing payments, and providing aftercare notes.
  • To personalise your experience — remembering therapist preferences, past treatments, and health notes so each visit feels tailored.
  • To send marketing communications — exclusive offers, seasonal promotions, and wellness tips via email or SMS, only if you have opted in. You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • To improve our services — analysing aggregated, anonymised data about how our website and services are used.
  • To comply with legal obligations — maintaining booking records as required under Indian financial and business regulations.
  • To manage safety and fraud prevention — protecting both our guests and our business from fraudulent activity.

We will never use your data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects without your explicit consent.

04 Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the limited circumstances below:

  • Service providers — We use trusted third-party platforms for appointment scheduling (e.g., booking software), email delivery (e.g., Mailchimp), and website analytics (e.g., Google Analytics). These providers are contractually bound to process data only on our behalf.
  • Payment processors — Payment transactions at our premises are handled via Razorpay or standard POS systems. We do not store full card details.
  • Legal compliance — We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or Indian regulatory authority.
  • Business transfers — In the unlikely event of a business acquisition, data would transfer to the new owner under the same privacy protections, and you would be notified.
International transfers: If any of our service providers process data outside India, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy assessments consistent with the DPDPA.

05 Data Retention

We keep your personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Booking records — Retained for 3 years from the date of your last appointment, in line with standard Indian business record-keeping practice.
  • Health consultation notes — Retained for the duration of your active client relationship and deleted within 6 months of your last visit, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Marketing preferences — Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Website analytics — Anonymised and aggregated after 14 months.
  • Enquiry / contact form submissions — Retained for 12 months and then securely deleted.

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

06 Your Rights

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 and applicable privacy principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw marketing consent at any time with immediate effect.

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Grieve

Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@serenityspa.in. We will respond within 30 days. There is no fee for a first request; we reserve the right to charge a reasonable administrative fee for manifestly excessive or repetitive requests.

07 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to ensure the site functions correctly and to understand how visitors use it. We use three categories of cookies:

Essential (always active)
Analytics (opt-in)
Marketing (opt-in)
  • Essential cookies — Necessary for the website to function (e.g., session management, booking form state). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand page visits, bounce rates, and user journeys via Google Analytics 4. Data is anonymised and not linked to individual identities.
  • Marketing cookies — Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign performance. Only activated if you accept marketing cookies via our cookie banner.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings link in the website footer, or through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the booking system.

08 Children's Privacy

Serenity Spa services are designed for adults (18 years and over). We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18.

If a parent or guardian believes their child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us immediately at privacy@serenityspa.in and we will promptly delete such data from our records.

For guest bookings that include minors as recipients of child-appropriate services (e.g., a mother-daughter wellness package), the consenting adult parent or guardian acts as the responsible Data Principal and provides consent on the minor's behalf.

09 Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website.
  • Access controls ensuring only authorised staff can access client records.
  • Regular staff training on data handling and confidentiality obligations.
  • Secure deletion protocols when data reaches the end of its retention period.
  • Third-party service provider security assessments before onboarding.
Data breach notification: In the unlikely event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights, we will notify you and the Data Protection Board of India within the timeframes required by the DPDPA — without undue delay.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Notify registered clients by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect.
  • Display a prominent notice on our website for 30 days following a material update.

Your continued use of our services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
