Bookings & Appointments 02

All appointments must be booked in advance either online, by phone, or in person. Bookings are subject to availability and are only confirmed upon receipt of a confirmation from our team.

  • Appointments for treatments lasting 60 minutes or more require a 50% deposit at the time of booking.
  • Clients are asked to arrive 10 minutes before their scheduled appointment to complete a consultation form.
  • Late arrivals may result in a reduced treatment time at the full scheduled price.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service where it is not appropriate for health or safety reasons.

Cancellations & Refunds 03

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • More than 48 hours notice: Full refund or free rescheduling.
  • 24–48 hours notice: 50% refund of deposit. Free rescheduling subject to availability.
  • Less than 24 hours notice: No refund of deposit. Rescheduling not available.
  • No-shows: Full session fee will be charged.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days to the original payment method.

Guest Conduct 04

Serenity Spa is a tranquil environment. We ask all guests to respect the peace and comfort of fellow guests and our therapists.

  • Mobile phones should be silenced or switched off in all treatment and relaxation areas.
  • Photography is not permitted in treatment rooms or changing areas.
  • We have a strict no-alcohol policy within the spa.
  • Any inappropriate behaviour will result in immediate termination of the visit with no refund issued.

Health & Safety 05

Your safety is our priority. Please disclose any medical conditions, allergies, or medications on your consultation form before receiving any treatment.

  • Certain treatments are not suitable for clients who are pregnant, have cardiovascular conditions, or have recent injuries.
  • We reserve the right to decline a treatment if we believe it poses a risk to the guest's health.
  • Serenity Spa accepts no liability for adverse reactions arising from undisclosed medical conditions.
